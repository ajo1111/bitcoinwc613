About Today
Why We're Here

Money has always been about power — who controls it, who distributes it, and who gets left out. Today we change that conversation.

Whether you've never heard of a wallet or you're already stacking sats — this event meets you where you are and walks you through Bitcoin as an asset, open source technology, and living currency built for the people.

Receive the currency. Distribute it to your community. Flow is the principle. Freedom is what it creates. Agency is what you do with it.
